Meet Toto InfoCare

Hello There! We offer training to nannies, pre-school teachers and care-givers on Nutritional Health and Practical First Aid skills. Our main aim is to promote home safety and nutritional health among children. We believe proper nutritional care is vital among children as it helps them not only in growth and development, but also enables them achieve greater milestones in life. It is also key to note that different ages require different nutrients due to their different body requirements.
